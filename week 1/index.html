GPUsurgeon

A GPU, or Graphics Processing Unit, is a specialized electronic component in a computer that's designed to handle tasks related to rendering images and graphics. It's like a supercharged processor that excels at performing complex mathematical calculations required for things like video games, 3D rendering, and other graphics-intensive applications. GPUs are known for their parallel processing capabilities, making them great for handling multiple tasks simultaneously, which is essential for smooth and fast graphics performance. In recent years, GPUs have also found applications in fields like artificial intelligence and scientific computing due to their ability to accelerate certain types of computations.
